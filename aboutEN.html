Wood windows and doors

Our company offers three-layer wooden joinery, developed on the basis of JAF profiles. The multi-layer glued profiles for joinery with visible wedge-shaped splicing are intended for the production of high-quality windows. They are also suitable for making door frames. The gluing of flawless slats ensures constant quality of the profiles.
